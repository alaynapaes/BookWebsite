About the Book

Set in the charming English countryside where gently rolling hills and serene meadows conceal secrets and laughter hides betrayals, this thrilling plot born from the vivid imaginations of two teenage authors from Goa, takes you on a roller-coaster filled with mystery, suspense, romance and drama leaving you guessing every step of the way. The story follows a series of mysterious events that test trust, reveal hidden motives and expose the thorns beneath the beauty of friendship and truth.

Written in vivid, cinematic language, Every Rose Has Its Thorns appeals to fans of mystery thrillers and young-adult fiction. Its relatable characters, emotional undertones, and cleverly layered twists make it perfect for readers seeking a compelling yet heartfelt narrative. This is really an impressive debut from two promising young storytellers.

About the Authors

Lisa Bragança and Carina Paes are two young, fun-loving teenagers from the picturesque state of Goa. Surrounded by serene beaches and lush green hills, they developed a love for mystery novels that soon turned into a passion for storytelling. Between schoolwork and seaside sunsets, they co-wrote Every Rose Has Its Thorns—a gripping tale born from imagination, curiosity, and friendship. Their fresh narrative voice captures both the innocence of youth and the thrill of suspense, inspiring readers to chase creativity fearlessly.
